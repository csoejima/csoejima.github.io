Education

On campus, I serve as Webmaster on the executive board for Slope Media Group, am a brother of Alpha Phi Omega, work as a lab operator for Cornell CIT (formerly at the Cornell Academic Technologies Center in the Student Technology Assistant Program), and am / have been a teaching assistant for the Introduction to and Intermediate Web Design & Development courses.

I've always had an interest in engineering and computer science, as well as psychology, communication, and cognitive science.
